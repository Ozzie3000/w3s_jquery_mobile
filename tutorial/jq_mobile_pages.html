    Example info
  • The data-role="page" is the page displayed in the browser
  • The data-role="header" creates a toolbar
  • The data-role="main" defines the content of the page such as: buttons, forms, images, text etc.
  • The "ui-content" class adds extra padding and margin inside the page content
  • The data-role="footer" creates a toolbar usually at the bottom of the page or wherever you put it
